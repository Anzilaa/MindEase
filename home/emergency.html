Emergency Contacts for Mental Wellness

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out to one of the following resources immediately:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (India): 9152987821
  • Vandrevala Foundation (India): 9152987821 / 9152987820
  • iCALL (TISS): 9152987821 (Mon-Sat, 10am-8pm)
  • Fortis Stress Helpline: +91 8376804102
  • Sumaitri (Delhi): 011-23389090
  • Sneha Foundation (Chennai): 044-24640050
  • COOJ (Goa): 0832-2252525
  • Helpline for Children (CHILDLINE): 1098
  • Police / Ambulance: 100 / 102

If you are outside India, please contact your local emergency number or a trusted mental health professional.

You are not alone. Help is available.
